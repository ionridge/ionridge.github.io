About

Built on Conviction

IonRidge is a Massachusetts-based portfolio management firm. We deploy proprietary scanning algorithms to continuously monitor equity markets, isolating statistically significant patterns and mispriced opportunities across thousands of securities in real time.

Every position is grounded in fundamental conviction and sized through a systematic risk framework. We pair concentrated equity exposure with disciplined options overlays to generate asymmetric return profiles while managing drawdown risk.

Approach

How We Invest

01

Automated Market Scanning

Proprietary algorithms continuously screen the equity universe for anomalous price behavior, volatility dislocations, and factor exposures — surfacing high-conviction opportunities that manual analysis would miss.

02

Fundamental Research

Scanner-generated signals are validated through deep bottom-up analysis — modeling unit economics, free cash flow durability, and competitive moat strength to build a conviction-weighted thesis for each position.

03

Systematic Options Overlay

We exploit implied volatility surfaces and term structure dynamics through defined-risk spreads, premium harvesting, and tactical hedges — constructing payoff profiles calibrated to each position's risk-reward parameters.

04

Risk Management

Portfolio-level exposure is monitored through real-time Greeks aggregation, correlation analysis, and scenario modeling. Position sizing follows a Kelly-informed framework to optimize geometric growth while capping tail risk.
